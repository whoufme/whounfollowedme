# whounfollowedme
Track who unfollowed you on Instagram. Simple &amp; private.
Who Unfollowed Me helps you track your Instagram followers and find out exactly who unfollowed you — without any third-party access to your account.

HOW IT WORKS
Simply download your data directly from Instagram (Settings → Your Activity → Download Your Information), upload the followers file to our app, and we'll show you exactly who unfollowed you between uploads.

FEATURES
- Unfollower Detection — See exactly who unfollowed you
- Follower Gains — Track who started following you
- Growth Chart — Visualize your follower count over time
- Daily & Weekly Reports — Understand your growth patterns
- Unfollow Pattern Analysis — See which days you lose the most followers
- Dark & Light Mode — Beautiful glassmorphism design
- 100% Private — We never access your Instagram account or password

YOUR PRIVACY COMES FIRST
We never ask for your Instagram password. We never connect to Instagram directly. All your data stays in your personal account — only you can see it.

IMPORTANT NOTE
This app uses Instagram's official data export feature. Follower tracking requires you to download your data from Instagram and upload it to the app. Instagram data exports can take up to 48 hours to prepare.

Who Unfollowed Me is not affiliated with or endorsed by Instagram or Meta.
