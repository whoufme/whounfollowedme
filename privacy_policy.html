Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We are committed to being transparent and keeping your data safe.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Email address — used for account creation and login only
  • Instagram username — the username you manually enter in the app
  • Follower data — data from your Instagram export file that you voluntarily upload to the app

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To detect changes in your follower list between uploads
  • To display follower statistics, reports, and insights in the app
  • To send you notifications about follower changes (only if enabled)

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT access your Instagram account directly
  • We do NOT store your Instagram password
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising
  • We do NOT share your data with anyone

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

Your follower data is stored only in your personal account and is protected by Row Level Security — only you can access your own data.

5. Instagram Data Export

Our app works by analyzing data from your personal Instagram data export — a file you download directly from Instagram and voluntarily upload to our app. We do not access Instagram's API or your account directly.

This export file contains your follower list. We store this data to compare across uploads and detect who unfollowed you.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us or using the "Delete Account" option in the app settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (Privacy Policy)

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
